# Features Developed for Specific Health Pillars

This document outlines the key features and functionalities developed for the `/health-employment`, `/health-ecosystems`, and `/health-entrepreneurship` sections of the Africa Health Collaborative (AHC) website.

---

## 1. Health Employment (`/health-pillars/health-employment`)

**Component:** `src/pages/healthPillars/HealthEmployment.tsx`

**Features:**
*   **Health Employment Overview:** Provides general information and context about AHC's health employment initiatives.
*   **Program Details:** Displays specific programs, projects, or partnerships related to fostering health employment.
*   **Impact and Outcomes:** Showcases the results and impact of health employment efforts.

---

## 2. Health Ecosystems (`/health-pillars/health-ecosystems`)

**Component:** `src/pages/healthPillars/HealthEcosystems.tsx`

**Features:**
*   **Health Ecosystems Overview:** Presents an overview of AHC's approach to developing and strengthening health ecosystems.
*   **Strategic Initiatives:** Details on strategic initiatives and collaborations aimed at improving health ecosystems.
*   **Resources for Ecosystem Development:** Information or links to resources supporting health ecosystem growth.

---

## 3. Health Entrepreneurship (`/health-pillars/health-entrepreneurship/*`)

This section is dedicated to Health Innovation and is built using a separate layout (`HILayout`) with multiple sub-pages.

**Components (from `src/pages/healthInnovation/`):**
*   `HIHome.tsx`
*   `HIPrograms.tsx`
*   `HIServices.tsx`
*   `HIAbout.tsx`
*   `HIBlog.tsx`
*   `HIContact.tsx`
*   `HIDemo.tsx`
*   `HIPrivacy.tsx`
*   `HITerms.tsx`
*   `VentureApplication.tsx`
*   `VentureShowcase.tsx`
*   `VentureDetail.tsx`
*   `ProgressUpdates.tsx`

**Features:**
*   **Health Innovation Home Page:** An introductory page for the health entrepreneurship and innovation section.
*   **Programs Listing:** Displays various programs and initiatives designed to support health entrepreneurs.
*   **Services Offered:** Details the services available to health innovators and ventures (e.g., mentorship, funding access).
*   **About Health Innovation:** Provides background, mission, and vision for the health innovation efforts.
*   **Innovation Blog/Articles:** A section for blog posts, news, and articles related to health entrepreneurship and innovation.
*   **Contact for Health Innovation:** Specific contact information or forms for inquiries related to health innovation.
*   **Demonstration/Showcase:** Pages potentially featuring live demos or detailed showcases of innovative health solutions.
*   **Privacy Policy:** Outlines the privacy practices specific to the health innovation platform.
*   **Terms and Conditions:** Details the terms of service for engaging with the health innovation programs or platform.
*   **Venture Application Form:** A dedicated form for entrepreneurs to apply for venture support or programs.
*   **Venture Showcase:** A gallery or listing of successful or promising health innovation ventures.
*   **Venture Detail Pages:** Individual pages providing in-depth information about specific health ventures.
*   **Progress Updates:** Regular updates on the development, milestones, and achievements of health innovation initiatives and supported ventures.
